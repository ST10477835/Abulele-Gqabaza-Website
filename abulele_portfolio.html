About Me

I completed both my primary and high school education at Ashbury College, where I consistently performed at the top of my class, earning top achiever awards in various subjects and ranking first among the top five students.

"Wisdom is not a product of schooling but of the lifelong attempt to acquire it." - Albert Einstein

This quote reflects my dedication to the pursuit of knowledge, which is one of the many reasons I was drawn to the field of computer science. I am a driven and hardworking individual, eager to grow, innovate, and contribute meaningfully in the tech world.
